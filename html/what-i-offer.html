THE COURSE

A flexible 6 to 8 week Hindi course

I offer a flexible course of 6 to 8 weeks, with one lesson per week. Each lesson runs for approximately 90 minutes. The full course is recommended for the best learning outcome, but the programme can be adapted to suit your existing knowledge and goals. Advanced lessons are also available for students who want to go further.

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

Every lesson is built around you

At the start of your first lesson, we'll discuss your current level and what you'd like to achieve. From there, your lessons are shaped around those goals - clearly, logically, and at your own pace. New to Hindi? Read Is Hindi hard to learn?

🗣

Speaking & Pronunciation

Build confidence in spoken Hindi from lesson one. You'll learn how sounds work and how to communicate clearly and naturally.

✍️

Reading & Writing

Learn to read and write Devnagri - the beautiful script used for Hindi. Many students are surprised by how quickly it comes.

📖

Basic Grammar

Hindi grammar is logical and structured. You'll learn the rules in a way that makes sense, so you can build sentences with confidence.

💬

Everyday Vocabulary

Practical words and phrases for real-life situations — travel, shopping, greetings, family, food, and more.

🔄

Conversation Practice

Guided conversation to help you think in Hindi, respond naturally, and build real fluency over time.

🎓

Advanced Hindi

For students returning after a course, or those who already have some foundation and want to go deeper.

YOUR OUTCOMES

What you'll be able to do

  1. 1️⃣
    After Lesson 1

    Write your name in Hindi script — and begin to understand how the language works logically.

  2. 🎓
    By the end of the course

    Read, write, speak, and understand everyday Hindi with real confidence. You'll also have techniques to support your continued learning independently.

  3. 🌏
    Ongoing

    The skills and tools to keep going — whether you're practising before a trip to India, watching Hindi films, or deepening your connection with family.
