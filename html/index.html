HINDI LEARNING QUESTIONS

Helpful answers before you begin

A few common questions from people thinking about private Hindi lessons in Wellington.

How long does it take to learn Hindi?

Most beginners start building useful phrases, pronunciation and script confidence within a short 6 to 8 week course. Your pace depends on your goals and practice time.

Read the beginner guide

Can beginners learn Hindi script?

Yes. Complete beginners are welcome, and lessons can include reading and writing Devanagari step by step alongside speaking and everyday vocabulary.

Start as a beginner

Is Hindi useful for travelling in India?

Hindi helps you connect more naturally in markets, transport, temples, homes and everyday conversations, especially beyond places where English is common.

Prepare for India travel

Do I need to know Hindi before lessons?

No prior Hindi is needed. Your first lessons are shaped around your current level, whether you are completely new or already know some words.
