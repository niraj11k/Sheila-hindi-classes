SERVICE AREA

Private Hindi tutoring from Brooklyn, Wellington

Sheila's Hindi Classes offers private Hindi lessons from Brooklyn, Wellington. Lessons are arranged directly with Anuradha, so you can discuss your current level, reason for learning and preferred times before you begin.

Learners come from across Wellington, including Brooklyn, Newtown, Karori, Kelburn, Te Aro, Mount Cook, Island Bay and nearby suburbs. If you are elsewhere in Wellington, you are welcome to get in touch and ask what arrangements may work.
