Your questions, answered

Pricing is not listed on this website — please get in touch directly to discuss. You can call or text Anuradha on 021 026 97038, or email hindiclass@xtra.co.nz. Discounts are available for students and Community Services Card holders.

Each lesson runs for approximately 90 minutes. Lessons are held once a week, and the standard course runs for 6 to 8 weeks. The format can be adapted depending on your goals and existing knowledge.

Hindi can feel unfamiliar at first, especially if you are new to Indian languages, but it becomes manageable when speaking, pronunciation, grammar and script are introduced step by step. Read the beginner guide to learning Hindi.

Not at all — complete beginners are very welcome. Anuradha will assess your level at the very first lesson and shape the course around where you are and where you want to go.

All learning materials are provided — you don't need to buy any textbooks or workbooks. Just bring a notebook and a pen to jot down notes, and you're all set.

Lessons are open to learners of all ages. If you'd like to discuss lessons for a child or young person, please get in touch and Anuradha will be happy to talk through the options.
